About Us
In 2001, we set out to establish a restaurant catering to each individual’s unique food and diet needs while keeping the restaurant itself as unique as the people it serves. Born from this premise came Something for Everyone, an innovative restaurant focusing on the individual dietary needs of its customers. We wanted to include meals on the menu that were healthy, of high-quality, cost effective, tasted as delicious as foods cooked at home, and at the same time, was prepared only with the ingredients you will eat. To accomplish this, thirteen separate menus have been individually customized to fit thirteen different types of diets. With thirteen separate areas in our restaurant’s kitchen; each area prepares food for that particular diet. Making sure you and your company are served with only foods everyone will enjoy is what Something for Everyone is all about.
